The Ultimate Guide To TWICE’s Official Merchandise Since Debut

  • Mac Doe
  • 1:33 pm
  • DEC 5, 2013

Top girl group TWICE debuted less than two years ago, they’ve already released a ton of merchandise.

Koreaboo recapped 16 of the rarest goods they’ve released so far, but TWICE has come out with a lot more than just 16 different types of goods. 

Here’s just about all of the official TWICE merchandise that’s been released so far: 

The Story Begins 

1 Mini Album (Korean Press)

2 Album Photocards (Red/Adult)

Each copy of The Story Begins comes with 1 of 9 random red photocards. 

3 Album Photocards (Pink/Baby)

The Pink Set of photocards featured two baby (or childhood) photos of each member, totaling 18 cards in the set.

4 Album Photocards (White/Sub-unit)

Each copy of The Story Begins comes with 1 of 3 sub-unit photocards. The units are: Nayeon-Sana-Tzuyu, Jihyo-Momo-Chaeyoung, and Mina-Dahyun-Jeongyeon.

5 Poster

6 CGV Postcards

TWICE teamed up with CGV theaters for a special TWICE Combo popcorn set to celebrate their debut. Fans who purchased one of these special sets received 1 Large “Banana Shake” Popcorn, 2 drinks, and a random TWICE postcard. Only the 10,000 customers received postcards!

7 “Like OOH-AHH” Broadcast Attendance Card

Fans who attended TWICE’s debut promotion broadcasts received an attendance card. Rewards were given out based on how many broadcasts were attended. 

8 Showcase Card

Fans who attended the “Like OOH-AHH” showcase received a special showcase postcard. 

9 Showcase Light Stick

Fans who attended the “Like OOH-AHH” showcase also received a special one-time use showcase lighstick. 

10 Mini Album (Thailand Press)

To date, all of TWICE’s albums (except TWICEcoaster: Lane 1 – [Christmas Edition]) have had Thailand versions. The Thailand press albums were smaller than the Korean press and had a different cover. 

11 Album Photocards (Thailand)

Each copy of The Story Begins comes with 1 of 9 random photocards. 

12 Poster (Thailand)

Each copy of The Story Begins (Thailand Edition) comes with 1 poster.

13 Mini Album (Taiwan Press)

The Story Begins was the only album to have an official Taiwan edition. The Taiwan press albums included a DVD and was slightly bigger than the Korean version. 

14 Album Photocard (Taiwan Press)

Every copy of the Taiwan edition of The Story Begin comes with a group photocard.

13 M-Wave Photocard

TWICE held a special signed album event with Mnet‘s M-Wave online shop. Fans who purchased copies of the album had a chance to win a rare polaroid-style photocard. 

Page Two

1 Mini Album (Korean Press)

2 Mini Album (Limited Edition)

Chaeyoung designed a special sleeve for a limited release.

3 Album Photocards (Selfie)

Each copy of Page Two comes with 1 of 9 random selfie photocards. 

4 Album Photocards (Flower)

Each copy of Page Two comes with 1 of 9 random flower photocards. 

5 Lenticular Cards

Each copy of Page Two comes with 1 of 9 lenticular cards. 

6 Album Photocards (Sub-unit)

Each copy of Page Two comes with 1 of 3 random sub-unit photocards. The sub-unit are Momo-Nayeon-Jeongyeon, Sana-Dahyun-Jihyo, and Mina-Chaeyoung-Tzuyu. 

7 Posters

Page Two has two different posters.

8 Showcase Card

Fans who attended the “Cheer Up” showcase received a special showcase postcard. 

9 Showcase Light Stick

Fans who attended the “Cheer Up” showcase also received a special one-time use showcase lighstick. 

10 Broadcast Cards

Broadcast cards were given out to fans who attended “Cheer Up” recordings at broadcast shows. 

8 Dunkin’ Donuts Sticker

To promote their first comeback, TWICE released special promotional stickers with Dunkin’ Donuts, and even worked at a store for an event! 

9 Broadcast Trophy 

This glass mini-trophy was given to fans at TWICE’s mini-fanmeeting to celebrate the success of their first comeback. 

10 Mini Album (Thailand Press)

11 Album Photocards (Thailand)

Each copy of Page Two (Thailand Edition) comes with 1 of 9 random photocards. 

12 Poster (Thailand)

Each copy of Page Two (Thailand Edition) comes with 1 poster.

13 M-Wave Photocards

Like with their debut, TWICE held a special signed album event with Mnet‘s M-Wave online shop. Fans who purchased copies of the album had a chance to win a rare polaroid-style photocard. This time, there was 3 different versions of the card.

14 Monograph

TWICEcoaster: Lane 1

1 Mini Album (Korean Press)

2 Album Photocards (Selfie)

Each copy of TWICEcoaster: Lane 1 comes with 1 of 9 random selfie photocards. 

3 Album Photocards (Holographic)

Each copy of TWICEcoaster: Lane 1 comes with 1 of 9 random holographic photocards. 

4 Album Photocards (Pre-Order)

Fans who pre-ordered TWICEcoaster: Lane 1 albums also received a bonus set of photocards. 

5 Super Card

1000 random Super Cards were included randomly in the first press copies of TWICEcoaster: Lane 1 .

6 Posters

There are two different posters of TWICEcoaster: Lane 1 , depending on the album purchased. 

7 Showcase Card

Fans who attended the “TT” showcase received a special showcase postcard. 

8 Broadcast Cards

Broadcast cards were given out to fans who attended “TT” recordings at broadcast shows.

6 Knock Knock Goods

7 Posters
