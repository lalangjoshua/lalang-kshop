A Beginner’s Guide to the Alternate Universe of BTS Outcast

  • Mac Doe
  • 1:33 pm
  • DEC 5, 2013

For all of us who saw the hashtag #BTSoutcast, and had many many questions.

Many of us woke up today to see #BTSOutcast trending worldwide on Twitter. Which then begged the questions did someone label BTS as outcasts? Did BTS just release a new track? WHAT WAS GOING ON?

So we did what any sane person would do next, click on the hashtag for some clarification on the matter.

Except that didn’t help and only propelled us further into this madness with more questions than we even had to begin with. Why is there a fake J-Hope? To sync or not sync?!

ADVERTISING
inRead invented by Teads

So we then managed to track down the original author of this “au” aka Alternate Universe and now have the full story for all those confused and lost individuals out there that just want to know wtf is going on.

The AU is called “BTS HORROR!AU” and follows the adventures of Hoseok (J-Hope) and Yoongi (Suga) as they navigate some weird game that controls real life.

The story begins with Hoseok telling Yoongi about a missing Park Jimin. To which brings us to the realization that in this universe the gang does not all know each other.

Cue the game! Suga tells Hoseok about a “fun” new game he found called Outcast! They were supposed to play together but Yoongi can’t wait for Hoseok to get home from his daycare job so he decided to crack the game open himself.

The game then asks him to input his phone number which he reluctantly does and the game begins with a text.

He then realizes that whatever decisions he makes on his phone, the characters on his screen complete.

Yoongi receives a mysterious message and tries to uninstall the game.

It’s then revealed that Yoonji is controlling the fate of Jimin’s character while Hoseok is controlling the fate of Jungkook’s character. But here’s the fun part, the readers get to join in too by voting in the first poll of the game to either intensify Yoongi’s or Hoseok’s game.

The next day, Yoongi and Hoseok try to piece what’s happening together.

And as we all patiently waited for more, fans began to have their own fan theories about the game and the killer.

People were getting sucked in, left and right

Even asking for RM to be brought in to solve the mystery

This one theory had us ALL shook…

Needless to say this was one wild ride and we can’t wait to see how it ends. We would like to propose that BigHit seriously considers making this a mini web drama!

Until then, we will all be stalking this twitter account for more updates.
